# Where this fits in the Founder OS

Use this after `ai-gtm-command-center` and `founder-led-sales-call-os` start producing account and call signals. Pair it with `startup-metrics-playbook` for metric definitions and `revops-infrastructure-playbook` for CRM and reporting architecture.

## Related modules

- `founder-os`: umbrella operating system and repo map.
- `founder-ai-workflow-roi-os`: company-wide AI leverage and hire-vs-automate decisions.
- `ai-gtm-command-center`: pre-call GTM research and outreach workflow.
- `founder-led-sales-call-os`: post-call sales intelligence and deal rescue.
- `founder-os-revenue-engine`: revenue leakage and weekly GTM diagnosis.
- `founder-weekly-operating-review-agent`: weekly operating review.
- `board-pack-investor-update-agent`: board and investor narrative.
- `revops-infrastructure-playbook`: CRM, reporting, handoff, and automation architecture.
- `startup-metrics-playbook`: metric definitions and operating interpretation.
- `founder-outreach-tracker`: relationship and follow-up tracking.

Link only the modules that matter for your workflow. Do not force every repo into every operating cadence.
