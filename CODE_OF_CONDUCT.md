# Code of Conduct

Keep contributions practical, respectful, and focused on improving founder/operator usefulness.

## Expected behavior

- Be specific and constructive.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality.
- Keep feedback tied to the repo's operating workflow.
- Avoid adding sensitive or proprietary data.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Sharing private data without permission.
- Misrepresenting what the repo does or where it has been used.
